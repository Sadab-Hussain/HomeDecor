Our Team

A home designer, also known as an interior designer or residential designer,
is a professional who specializes in creating functional and aesthetically pleasing interior spaces for homes. These professionals work closely with clients to understand their preferences, lifestyle, and requirements, and then use their expertise to design spaces that meet these needs. Here's a general description of a home designer.

Kelly Wearstler

Renowned for her bold and eclectic designs, Kelly Wearstler is an American interior designer known for combining luxury and functionality. Her work often features a mix of textures, colors, and unique art pieces, creating visually striking and personalized spaces.

Philippe Starck

A celebrated French designer, Philippe Starck is known for his avant-garde and innovative approach to design. His creations often blend functionality with artistic flair, resulting in modern and imaginative living spaces. Starck's designs frequently incorporate sustainable and eco-friendly elements.

Patricia Urquiola

Spanish-born designer Patricia Urquiola is recognized for her elegant designs. Her work combines traditional craftsmanship with modern aesthetics,in interiors that are both functional and visually appealing. Urquiola's designs reflect a balance between innovation and timelessness.

Kelly Hoppen

An Italian fashion designer, Giorgio Armani is known for his sophisticated and timeless designs. His menswear collections often feature tailored suits, clean lines, and luxurious fabrics, reflecting a classic yet modern aesthetic. He specializes in creating elegant and culturally rich spaces.



Krishna Mehta

Krishna Mehta is a versatile designer known for his expertise in both fashion and interior design. His interior designs often reflect a blend of traditional Indian craftsmanship with contemporary aesthetics. He specializes in creating elegant and culturally rich spaces.

Rohit Bal

While primarily known as a fashion designer, Rohit Bal has also ventured into interior design. His designs are luxurious, often blending traditional Indian aesthetics with modern sensibilities. His work is marked by opulent details and a keen sense of artistry.

Pinakin Patel

Pinakin Patel is a renowned Indian interior designer and architect recognized for his contemporary designs.His work often features clean lines,and a focus on natural materials.Patel's designs emphasize simplicity ,creating spaces that exude a sense of modernity.

Ritu Nanda

Ritu Nanda was a respected Indian interior designer celebrated for her elegant and timeless designs. She was known for her ability to create luxurious and comfortable living spaces, incorporating a harmonious balance of colors,and furniture to bring sophistication to every project
